Eligibility Criteria

Course Minimum Qualification Entrance Exam Min. Marks
Engineering (B.Tech) 10+2 (PCM) JEE Main & Advanced 75% (PCM)
Medical (MBBS) 10+2 (PCB) NEET 50% (PCB)
Law (LLB) 10+2 (Any Stream) CLAT / AILET 45% (General)
Arts/Science (BA/BSc) 10+2 (Any Stream) CUET / DUET 60% (Varies)
