Student Visa for India

Indian Student Visa (for Nepalese)

Visa Type: Student Visa (S-1)

Processing Time: 2-4 weeks

Fee: NPR 3,500 (approx.)

Requirements:

  • Admission letter from recognized Indian institution
  • Passport valid for 6+ months
  • 4 passport-size photographs
  • Academic certificates (attested)
  • Financial proof (bank statement)
Indian Embassy in Nepal

Important Tips:

  • No visa required for SAARC quota seats
  • Medical check-up may be required
  • Register at FRRO within 14 days of arrival

F-1 Visa (USA)

F-1 Student Visa Process

Visa Type: F-1 Student Visa

Processing Time: 3-5 months

Fee: $510 (approx. NPR 61,000)

Requirements:

  • I-20 form from US university
  • Valid passport
  • DS-160 confirmation page
  • SEVIS fee payment receipt
  • Financial documents (1 year expenses)
  • TOEFL/IELTS scores
US Embassy Nepal

Application Steps:

  1. Get admission in SEVP-approved school
  2. Pay SEVIS fee ($350)
  3. Complete DS-160 form
  4. Schedule visa interview
  5. Attend interview at US Embassy

UK Student Visa (Tier 4)

Tier 4 (General) Student Visa

Visa Type: Tier 4 (General)

Processing Time: 3 weeks

Fee: £348 (approx. NPR 51,000)

Requirements:

  • CAS (Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies)
  • Valid passport
  • Financial proof (£1,265/month for London)
  • English proficiency (IELTS/PTE)
  • TB test certificate
UK Visa Official Site

Financial Requirements:

  • Outside London: £1,023/month (up to 9 months)
  • Inside London: £1,265/month (up to 9 months)
  • Must show funds for 28 consecutive days

Australian Student Visa

Student Visa (Subclass 500)

Visa Type: Subclass 500

Processing Time: 1-3 months

Fee: AUD 630 (approx. NPR 51,000)

Requirements:

  • Confirmation of Enrollment (CoE)
  • Genuine Temporary Entrant (GTE) statement
  • Financial proof (AUD 21,041/year)
  • English proficiency (IELTS 6.0+)
  • Health insurance (OSHC)
Australian Visa Info

Work Rights:

  • During studies: 40 hrs/fortnight
  • During holidays: Unlimited hours
  • Post-study work: 2-4 years (depending on qualification)

General Visa Application Process

1

University Admission

Get acceptance letter from recognized institution

2

Prepare Documents

Academic records, financial proof, passport, etc.

3

Online Application

Fill visa application form (DS-160, Tier 4, etc.)

4

Pay Fees

Visa application fee + SEVIS/IHS if applicable

5

Biometrics & Interview

Visit visa center for biometrics and interview

6

Visa Decision

Typically takes 2-8 weeks depending on country

Common Documents Checklist:

  • Passport (valid 6+ months)
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Admission letter
  • Academic certificates
  • English test scores
  • Financial proof (bank statements/sponsorship)
  • Visa application form
  • Medical reports (if required)
